Spam

I would like for Helsinki to have a Code & Coffee and/or a Purescript meetup. Please contact me if you have any interest in providing space and co-hosting.

For those wondering what the hell Code & Coffee meetups are:

  • They usually involve meeting every 2nd or 3rd Saturday from roughly 9 or 10AM to 1PM, give or take an hour or two.
  • The organizers usually have everyone come in a circle to say their name and what project they will be working on (e.g. learning Haskell).
  • Any sponsor ads (e.g. who brought donuts) are given here with a few ads about people who are hiring or looking for work.
  • The rest of the meetup is largely done alone or in small groups that might center around a project.
